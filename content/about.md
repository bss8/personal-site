## I Build Systems That Produce Measurable Results

I am a Staff Software Engineer building enterprise platforms and distributed systems that produce measurable impact. 
My work focuses on eliminating waste at scale, increasing engineering throughput, and hardening production environments.

Most recently, I designed and built the HPC Toolkit end-to-end: architecture, code, deployment, and adoption.
It now serves over 1,400 engineers and has delivered ~$12M in annual cost reduction.
It was featured in a company-wide article to 160,000 employees.

I operate across application, infrastructure, and platform layers, including Kubernetes, CI/CD systems, 
graph databases, and emerging AI orchestration patterns.

I move quickly and drive problems to resolution - I listen first, align on the real objective, then move decisively. 
I do not let important initiatives stall, and I take ownership of outcomes rather than tasks.

If a system introduces friction, duplication, or delay, I redesign it. 
If a platform can be standardized and made reusable, I build the foundation. 
The goal is durable systems that scale across teams and produce compounding value.
